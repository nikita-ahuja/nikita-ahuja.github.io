hi, im nikita!

i like to find gaps in our world and try to fill them. that's why i'm a developer by day & explorer by night. i’m also a runner, but you’ll only see me chasing things i’m passionate about.

i received my bachelor's degree from ut austin's mccombs school of business in 2017. in my time at ut, i was heavily involved with camp kesem and served on the board of the texas chapter as the volunteer coordinator. (*if you're familiar with kesem, my camp name is mushu!)

after graduating, i attended dev bootcamp where i gained experience as a fullstack developer. my love for code stems from my persistent curiosity with art, design, and functionality in a variety of contexts. when i'm not playing on my computer, you can find me rock climbing, running, cooking, or learning something new.
